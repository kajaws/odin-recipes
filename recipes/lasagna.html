Lasagna

This recipe is a special homemade lasagna with a flavorful, from-scratch tomato sauce and a rich, cheesy filling. Instead of the traditional ricotta, it features a unique blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan, creating a delicious combination.

It pairs wonderfully with a fresh green salad and crusty garlic bread.


Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time: Servings:
1hr 25min 1hr 15min 2hrs 50min 8

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1,5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  3. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  4. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  6. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 30-40 minutes. Remove the foil and continue bakin for about 5-10 minutes, until the top is golden brown.
